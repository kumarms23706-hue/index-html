India vs Australia Highlights, Women’s World Cup Semifinal: Jemimah Rodrigues’ ton helps INDW beat AUSW by 5 wickets, reach WC final.

India beat Australia by five wickets to reach the finals of ICC Women's World Cup 2025 final at DY Patil Stadium in Navi Mumbai on Thursday.

A century for ages by Jemimah Rodrigues led the India women’s team to a historic five-wicket win at the DY Patil Sports Academy in Navi Mumbai over Australia, as they qualify for the ICC Women’s World Cup 2025 final. Earlier, Pheobe Litchfield’s fiery ton alongside half-centuries from Ellyse Perry and Ash Gardner powered Australia to a mammoth 338 in 49.5 overs and set India the highest-ever target in a World Cup knockout match.

Women's cricket prepares to crown a new world champion!

The two finalists at this World Cup have crossed many barriers in the last decade or so. South Africa pulled themselves together after early struggles, while India made it to the knockouts despite losses to top teams. Now both nations will compete for their first ever ICC Women's World Cup title!
